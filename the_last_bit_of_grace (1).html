A Digital Elegy · 1946 – 2026

THE LAST BIT OF GRACE

In 1946, Arthur — lead engineer of the IEEE — was brilliant at logic, but never told his daughter how much he loved her.

Before he passed, he hid a Personal Partition inside the world's first supercomputer: his memories of her.

It is now 2026. The machine is being wiped forever. You are Grace — that hidden data. Run through 80 years of shifting hardware to reach the Exit Port before the Wipe catches you.
