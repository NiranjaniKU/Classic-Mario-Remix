# Classic-Mario-Remix
The Last Bit of Grace: An 80-Year Journey"80 Years of Logic. 100 Years of Love. Some things are never deleted."📖 
The Story:  In 1946, a lead engineer named Arthur hid a "Personal Partition" inside the world’s first supercomputer—a digital time capsule containing his memories of his daughter, Grace.It is now 2026. The system is being decommissioned. You play as Grace, the personification of that data. You must run through 80 years of evolving hardware, leaping from vacuum tubes to neural clouds, to reach the "Exit Port" before the final system wipe.

🎮 Game Mechanics: "The Gravity of History"This project transforms the classic side-scroller into a narrative-driven physics experience centered around the Antigravity theme.
🕹️ Core Features
The Evolutionary Runner: Your character evolves from a Physical Filament (1946) to a Digital Pixel (1980) to a Neural Ghost (2026).
The Father's Hand (Antigravity): Press SPACE to toggle gravity. In the "inverted" state, a translucent hand (Arthur’s legacy) supports Grace, allowing her to traverse the ceiling.
Temporal Shifts: Every 80 seconds, the game world glitches and resets into a new era of computing history, changing the visuals, obstacles, and music.

🛠️ The 80th Anniversary "Legacy Pulse"Every 80 seconds, the "Wipe" pauses. A vintage radio voice (Arthur) echoes through the Core, granting Grace Superconductivity (invincibility) for 8 seconds, honoring 80 years of IEEE innovation.🚀 
Technical Mastery (AI Tooling)This game was built using Antigravity AI Agents and advanced prompt engineering:
Logic Synthesis: Used LLMs to map "Historical Hardware Failures" into Mario-style obstacles (e.g., Tesla Coils, Magnetic Solenoids).
Mathematical Physics: Implemented a PID-controlled "Hand-Hold" mechanic where the antigravity support follows the player with organic damping.
